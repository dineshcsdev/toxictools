About the Project

In a world of sugarcoated feedback, ToxicTools offers a refreshing dose of honesty, wrapped in wit. We're building a platform where AI provides humorous, direct responses, helping you see things from a different, often hilarious, perspective.

Built with Flask and powered by OpenRouter API, our architecture is designed for privacy and respect. We process your requests in real-time without storing personal data.

This is an open-source initiative. We welcome issues, pull requests, and discussions from a vibrant community passionate about ethical AI and creative expression.

What You’ll Get

  • Honest Feedback: Unfiltered, witty responses that cut through the noise.
  • Fun Creativity: A unique way to engage with AI for entertainment and new perspectives.
  • Multilingual Edge: Support for English, Tamil, and Tanglish, embracing diverse cultures.
  • Zero-Cost Ethos: Free to use, built on open-source principles, accessible to everyone.
